Hi, I'm Manthan

I'm currently finishing up my MSc Science, Technology, and Policy at ETH Zürich, in the Department of Humanities, Social and Political Sciences .

My previous education has placed me at the intersection of computer- and data-science, and the social sciences. Coming from an interdisciplinary BSc programme, I have continued in a similar vein.

Throughout my master's education, I have developed an interest in thinking about matters of social justice in the "Age of AI," focusing specifically on social outcomes in labour and education. You can find relevant pieces I have written on topics I am interested in!

I am generally passionate about language and have been fascinated with NLP tools, and recently also LLMs as a new advent for us to make sense of all the language-data that our society produces. I am of a passionate conviction that these tools can find relevant, well-designed, and overall thoughtful uses in education and public politics. If you have ideas you would like to share or just chat with me about these things, feel free to write me an email!
